# Diners & Dungeons & Drive-ins & Dragons & Dives

You are standing on a large stone raised platform consisting of four large kitchens. The platform is the center of a giant arena full of spectators from all over the kingdom to see which team will succeed this year’s challenge. 

Between your platform and the audience is a sunken maze the top of which you can just see over. 

At the northernmost part of the audience is a grand viewing box in which sits Queen Julia Child the XIV, the judges, and the Queen’s adviser; a mysterious sorcerer known only as “The Chairman.”

The sun is just peeking over the eastern edge of the stadium as it climbs, casting shadows all over the maze before you.
